What is a Sustainable Farm?

“Sustainable agriculture can be defined in many ways, but it ultimately seeks to preserve farmers resources.” - Westernsare.com

A Sustainable Farm is a new and innovative way of growing plants. The practice of sustainable farms was introduced during the Great Depression when produce was hard to come by and had many waiting in bread lines.

    Farmers Define a Sustainable Farm to be of three things
  1. Economically Viable: If it turns a profit for the farmer
  2. Socially Viable: If it improves the quality of life for owners
  3. Ecologically Sound: If it uses less resources than the traditional method
